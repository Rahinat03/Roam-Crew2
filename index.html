Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

At RoamCrew, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and store information when you use our website or services, in compliance with the Ghana Data Protection Act and global best practices.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us via our forms (Contact, Join the Crew, Event Booking). This includes:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone / WhatsApp number
  • Company name (if applicable)
  • Trip interests and event preferences

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide curated trip proposals.
  • To process and manage your event bookings.
  • To communicate with you regarding updates, loyalty rewards, or new drop alerts (only if you opted in).

3. Data Security and Sharing

We implement standard security measures to protect your data. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties. Your data is only shared with essential service providers (e.g., our secure database service, SheetDB) solely for the purpose of storing your submissions.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:
